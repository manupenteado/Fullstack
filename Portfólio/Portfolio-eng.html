About me

Hello! I'm Emanuele Penteado, a Computer Engineering student and researcher at Senai Cimatec University.
I'm currently in my sixth semester and developing two scientific research projects at the university's Optics Lab. The first involves creating an optical fiber-based sensor to detect leaks in gas pipelines. The second is a study aimed at identifying a geometry that, when applied to optical fiber, enables a linear relationship with the macrobending phenomenon.
Computing, particularly Python, has been an essential tool for processing data in both projects. This technology has significantly aided me in data manipulation and analysis, which deeply excites me, as I believe computing is a powerful ally in pursuing innovative and efficient solutions.
